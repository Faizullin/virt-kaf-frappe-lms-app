Our History

Virt Kaf was founded in 2024 by a passionate group of educators, developers, and dreamers who envisioned a world where quality learning is not bound by geography or cost. Starting with a handful of video lessons, we've grown into a global platform trusted by thousands of learners across 50+ countries.

From our humble beginnings in a co-working space to building one of the most dynamic online learning ecosystems, our journey has been driven by a single mission: to empower every learner to achieve their full potential.

Our Mission & Vision
